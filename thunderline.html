How it works
The primitives

Six guarantees, inherited for free.

A station built on Thunderline doesn't bolt governance on afterward. It's born with it. These are the runtime's non-negotiables — every business above them gets them by default.

Identity

Durable identity

Every actor — human, agent, station — has a stable, addressable identity that persists across restarts.

Memory

Evented memory

State changes are events, not overwrites. The past is never silently rewritten.

Authority

Policy-gated action

No action runs until a policy gate admits it. Capability, actor and scope are checked at the membrane — fail-closed.

Evidence

Receipts

Every state transition writes a durable, content-hashed receipt. The record is the source of truth.

Provenance

Lineage

Receipts link to the actions that produced and caused them — a traceable graph of what led to what.

Proof

Replay

Reconstruct any sequence exactly, and verify it against its receipts. Autonomy you can re-run.

One action, end to end

Authorized before. Receipted after.

01 · intent

An agent proposes an action

AI prepares, routes, explains and recommends — it never acts on its own authority.

02 · gate

The policy gate decides

Capability, actor and scope are checked at the membrane. Admitted, or refused — and either way, recorded.

03 · seal

The runtime writes a receipt

Content-hashed, lineage-linked, durable. The moment the action lands, the evidence lands with it.

04 · replay

Anyone authorized can replay it

Reconstruct exactly what happened and why — for an operator, an auditor, or a regulator.
