A new standard in recovery

Targeted cold therapy without the downtime.

CryoH2 uses the SubZero system to deliver precise localized cryotherapy. Unlike traditional ice packs that cool only the surface, targeted treatment rapidly cools the treatment area and may help support the body's natural recovery response.

Sessions are efficient, non-invasive, and tailored to your goals, whether you are training, recovering, managing discomfort, or simply trying to move with greater ease.

How localized cryotherapy works

The SubZero system uses pressurized carbon dioxide vapor at approximately -110 degrees F to rapidly lower skin temperature and initiate a thermal shock response.

  • Temporary pain relief
  • Reduced inflammation and swelling
  • Improved mobility and function
  • Neuromuscular and tissue recovery support
  • Lymphatic drainage and circulation response support
