Why it's credible

The moat is the evidence envelope.

Public data is a commodity — anyone can search it. The differentiator is how each opportunity is packaged: a fixed review contract where every fact is traceable, every gap is visible, and the generator holds its confidence to what it actually verified.

Public-source · source-cited · fully receipted · no invented figures.
C1

A fixed 10-part packet

Same sections every time — a non-technical reviewer finds priorities and next actions in under thirty seconds.

C2

A graded evidence ledger

Source URLs, whether the notice and attachments were fetched and parsed, and a receipt id — you see how deep verification went.

C3

A fail-closed floor

Eligibility, pricing, and award probability stay marked for human review until proven — the packet holds its confidence to the evidence in hand.
