Why it's credible

The moat is the evidence envelope.

Public data is commodity — anyone can search it. The differentiator is how each opportunity is packaged: a fixed review contract where every fact is traceable and every gap is visible, and a generator tested to be unable to fake confidence.

Contract

A fixed 10-part packet

Same sections every time — a non-technical reviewer finds priorities and next actions in under 30 seconds.

Provenance

A graded evidence ledger

Source URLs, whether the notice & attachments were fetched and parsed, a receipt id — you see how deep verification went.

Guardrail

A fail-closed floor

Tested to be unable to claim eligibility, pricing, or award probability it didn't verify. It cannot fake confidence.
